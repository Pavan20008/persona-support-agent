# Cookie Clearing Guide

## When to Clear Cookies
Clear browser cookies when the Adsparkx dashboard fails to load, sessions expire unexpectedly, or you see stale UI elements after an update.

## Chrome
1. Open Settings > Privacy and security > Clear browsing data.
2. Select "Cookies and other site data."
3. Choose "Last hour" or "All time" depending on how long the issue has persisted.
4. Click "Clear data" and reload https://app.adsparkx.io.

## Firefox
1. Open Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data.
2. Click "Clear Data" and check "Cookies and Site Data."
3. Confirm and restart the browser.

## Safari
1. Safari menu > Settings > Privacy > Manage Website Data.
2. Search for "adsparkx.io" and remove all entries.
3. Reload the application.

## After Clearing Cookies
You will need to log in again. If two-factor authentication is enabled, have your authenticator app ready.

If the interface still fails to load after clearing cookies, try a private/incognito window to rule out extension conflicts.
