ADSPARKX BILLING POLICY
========================

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. BILLING CYCLE
All subscriptions are billed monthly or annually on the anniversary of signup.
Invoices are generated on the 1st of each month and payment is due within 14 days.

2. PAYMENT METHODS
We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), ACH bank transfer (Enterprise plans),
and wire transfer for annual contracts over $10,000 USD.

3. DUPLICATE CHARGES
If you believe you were charged twice for the same billing period:
- Check whether you have multiple active subscriptions under different team members.
- Review pending authorizations vs. settled charges in your billing portal.
- Duplicate charges are typically resolved within 5-7 business days after verification.

4. REFUND POLICY
- Monthly plans: Pro-rated refunds available within 14 days of charge for unused service.
- Annual plans: Refunds considered on a case-by-case basis for service outages exceeding SLA.
- Refund requests involving disputed charges require manual review by our billing team.

5. BILLING DISPUTE RESOLUTION TIMELINE
Standard billing disputes are investigated within 3-5 business days.
Complex cases involving multiple invoices or enterprise contracts may take 7-10 business days.
Executive accounts receive priority review within 2 business days.

6. OPERATIONAL IMPACT
Unresolved billing issues do not suspend API access during the investigation window
unless fraud is suspected. Service continuity is maintained for verified enterprise accounts.

7. CONTACT
Billing inquiries: billing@adsparkx.io
Include account ID, invoice number, and charge date for fastest resolution.
