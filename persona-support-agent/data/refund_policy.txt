REFUND AND CHARGEBACK POLICY
=============================

Refund Eligibility
------------------
Monthly subscriptions: Full refund within 14 days if no significant usage occurred.
Annual subscriptions: Pro-rated refunds for documented service failures only.
Usage is measured by API calls exceeding 1,000 or active integrations.

Chargeback Process
------------------
If you initiated a chargeback with your bank, our billing team will pause account changes
until the dispute is resolved. Contact billing@adsparkx.io immediately to expedite review.

Duplicate Charge Resolution
---------------------------
Verified duplicate charges are reversed within 5-7 business days.
Temporary credits may be applied within 24 hours for enterprise accounts.

Non-Refundable Items
--------------------
- Custom development fees
- One-time setup fees after 30 days
- Third-party marketplace purchases

Escalation
----------
Refund demands involving legal threats or regulatory complaints are routed to our
Compliance team. Response within 1 business day.
