# Subscription Management

## Viewing Your Plan
Dashboard > Billing > Subscription shows your current plan, renewal date, and seat count.

## Upgrading
1. Click "Change Plan" on the subscription page
2. Select Pro or Enterprise
3. Confirm payment method
4. Upgrades take effect immediately; billing is pro-rated

## Downgrading
Downgrades apply at the end of the current billing cycle.
Features above the new plan limit are disabled gracefully with a 7-day grace period.

## Canceling
1. Billing > Subscription > Cancel Subscription
2. Confirm cancellation
3. Access continues until the period ends
4. Data export is available for 30 days post-cancellation

## Seat Management
Enterprise admins can add/remove seats from Admin > Team.
Removed seats take effect at the next billing cycle unless otherwise specified.
