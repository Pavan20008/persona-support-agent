ADSPARKX SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
========================================

Uptime Commitment
-----------------
Enterprise plans guarantee 99.9% monthly uptime for the Adsparkx API and dashboard.
Downtime is measured when core services return HTTP 5xx for more than 5 consecutive minutes.

Operational Impact Reporting
----------------------------
Business customers receive automated incident reports within 1 hour of confirmed outages.
Reports include: affected services, estimated user impact, root cause summary, and remediation timeline.

Billing Dispute Resolution Timeline
------------------------------------
- Standard disputes: 3-5 business days
- Enterprise priority: 2 business days
- Executive escalation queue: same business day acknowledgment, resolution within 24-48 hours

Credit Policy
-------------
If monthly uptime falls below 99.9%, eligible enterprise accounts receive service credits
calculated as 10% of monthly fees per 0.1% below the SLA threshold.

Communication Channels
----------------------
Executive accounts have a dedicated account manager and 24/7 priority hotline.
Incident status page: https://status.adsparkx.io
